Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 4, 2026

At Lexicon Spell Bee, we are committed to providing a safe and educational environment for students and wordsmiths of all ages. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

We believe in "Privacy by Design." We do not require an email address or personal identity to play. We only collect:

  • Username: A name you choose to represent yourself on the leaderboard.
  • Game Data: Your scores and the levels you play.

2. Data Usage

Your username and score are used exclusively for the Live Leaderboard. This data is stored temporarily in our server memory and is not sold or shared with third-party advertisers.

3. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Lexicon is an educational tool. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we do not require registration or personal IDs, we are compliant with the basic principles of COPPA.

4. Third-Party Services

We use external services like DictionaryAPI.dev to provide word definitions. These services do not receive any of your individual user data from our app.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository.
